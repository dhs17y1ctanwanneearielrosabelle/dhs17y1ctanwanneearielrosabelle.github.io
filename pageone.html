Real Steel

Movie Review by Ariel Tan

Review

I would give Real Steel five out of five stars. It is an entertaining and thrilling movie, filled with actions. It also inculcates good moral values like perseverance and determination. The movie is suitable for both adults and children. I definitely would recommend Real Steel to others and to those who have not watched this movie, grab the chance to watch it for you will not regret!
